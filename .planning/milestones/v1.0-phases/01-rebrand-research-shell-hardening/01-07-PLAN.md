<objective>
Build the interaction-log service: a whitelisted event/Q&A constructor that snapshots the immutable identity internally, rejects prohibited fields, revisions Q/A records separately from events, writes durably to `research_records`, and enqueues each for upload. This is the client authoring layer for LOG-01 and locked decisions D-07/D-08/D-09 — the complete §14.1 logging contract that must exist before any personalization.

Purpose: Centralizing identity snapshotting and the field allowlist makes it structurally impossible to attach the wrong condition or a prohibited field.
Output: interaction-log.service.ts + a TDD suite covering the full event allowlist, privacy rejection, and Q/A revisioning through the seam.
</objective>
