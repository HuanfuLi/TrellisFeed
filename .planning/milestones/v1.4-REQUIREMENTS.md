## v1.4 Requirements Model

v1.4 phases use **phase-internal decision IDs** (D-01..D-47, TD-04/05/06, GAP-1..6, SEAM-11/12, named items like STARTER-PERSIST/CLEAR-RELOAD/PERF-MEMO/COSMETIC-POLISH) rather than the v1.1-style cross-phase REQ-IDs (FEED/IMAGE/PLANNER/NAV/CARDS/GRAPH).

This was deliberate per the audit: v1.4 was a polish + gap-closure milestone, not a new feature surface. Each phase tracks its own scope through CONTEXT.md decisions and surfaces verification through phase-internal IDs.
