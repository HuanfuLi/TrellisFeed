<objective>
Make the Ask-screen Send button send on the FIRST tap while the keyboard is open. Root cause:
Send is `<button type="submit">` relying on click/form-submit (fires on pointer-up, after focus
change); on Android WebView the first tap's blur dismisses the keyboard and the submit never
reaches handleSubmit. Fix: fire send on `onPointerDown` with `e.preventDefault()` to preserve
focus, sharing the send body with the form's Enter-key submit.

Purpose: One tap on Send sends; no need to dismiss the keyboard and tap again.
Output: Shared submitMessage() called from both onPointerDown and form onSubmit; source-guard test.
</objective>
