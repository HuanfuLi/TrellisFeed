<objective>
Stop provider/locale switches from corrupting already-generated text-art posts. Two independent
fixes: (1) raise the text-art Gemini token budget and/or disable thinking so the visible text is
not starved by thinking tokens; (2) treat persisted post content as immutable — reject empty/
fragment regenerations and never overwrite a non-empty `textArtContent` with a shorter one.

Purpose: Existing text-art posts that showed full sentences keep their full text and never
collapse to "T" / "Is your" after a switch to a Gemini thinking model + locale change.
Output: Reject gate + persist-merge guard in concept-feed.service.ts; Gemini budget/thinkingConfig
in providers/llm/index.ts; regression tests.
</objective>
