## Gaps

- [ISSUE] Redundant "Suggested Moves" section: There are two "Suggested Moves" sections in the Planner. One appears after the daily check-in (only when autogenerated) and lacks the "Skip All" button. The other is the standard section below "Continue". This redundancy causes confusion and prevents the "Skip All" feature from being accessible in the first section.
- [ISSUE] Refresh button accessibility: The "Refresh" button is only present in the first "Suggested Moves" section. If that section is empty or hidden (e.g., after dismissing all moves), the user cannot manually trigger a refresh from the Planner screen.
