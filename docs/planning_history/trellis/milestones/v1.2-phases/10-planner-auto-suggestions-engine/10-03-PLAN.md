# Plan 10-03: UAT Gap Closure — Unified Suggested Moves Section

## Objective

Fix 2 UAT failures (Tests 6 and 7) by merging the duplicate "Suggested Moves" sections in PlannerScreen into one unified section with always-accessible Refresh and Skip All controls.

**Root cause:** PlannerScreen renders two separate sections labeled "Suggested Moves":
1. Auto-Suggested (top, conditional on `autoMoves.length > 0`, has Refresh + Skip All hidden inside collapse)
2. Manual Suggested (bottom, always rendered via `SectionHeader`, no controls)

**Fix:** Merge into one unified "Suggested Moves" section showing both auto-moves (`autoMoves`) and manual suggested chunks (`suggestedChunks`), with Refresh and Skip All always visible in the header.
