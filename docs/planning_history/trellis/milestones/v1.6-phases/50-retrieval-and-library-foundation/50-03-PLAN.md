| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-50-QUOTA | Denial of Service | localStorage.setItem | accept | Silent drop on quota exceeded (matches engagementService and postHistoryService precedent). Real-world risk is low; user will notice missing data and Clear-All-Data resolves. |
